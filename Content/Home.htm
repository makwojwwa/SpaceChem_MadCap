SpaceChem Beginner Guide

Welcome to the SpaceChem Beginner Guide It is dedicated to the critically acclaimed computer game, SpaceChem. If you are new to the game and want to know what it is all about and what to expect, you are at the right place. Of course, you will also find here many useful advanced tips that will help you become better at the game. Everyone is invited!

Introduction

This is a general section describing the game, its structure and goals to be accomplished.

Game mechanics

This section presents the building blocks of the game: reactors The primary workspace in SpaceChem. This is where you use instructions to transform input atoms/molecules into output atoms/molecules. It is divided into input area, output area and remaining area., their components, and instructions A building block for algorithms in reactors. Must be placed inside the reactor along the path of a waldo so that it can be executed..

Gameplay

Here you will learn about types of assignments This is a specialised term used in the game for a game level. There are research, production, and defense assigments. you have to complete and about different environments where you actually do that.

Troubleshooting

Sometimes something can go wrong and you don't know why. In this chapter you will find out the causes of common problems and get useful tips that will help you become better at the game.
